About Us

Welcome to Charity Chess, where the love of chess meets the passion for giving back! Founded in 2008, our club is more than just a place to play chessâ€”it's a community dedicated to making a positive impact through the game we all cherish.

At Charity Chess, we believe that chess is a powerful tool for education, personal growth, and social change. Our mission is to bring the benefits of chess to everyone, especially those in underprivileged communities. Through our programs, we provide free chess lessons, organize community tournaments, and distribute chess sets to schools and organizations in need.

We are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment for players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or a seasoned player eager to give back, there's a place for you at Charity Chess. Our members are united by a shared love of the game and a desire to use our collective knowledge and resources to support charitable causes locally and globally.

Join us in our mission to make a difference, one move at a time. Together, we can change lives through the power of chess.

Our Vision

A world where everyone, regardless of background, has access to the educational and personal benefits of chess.

Our Mission

To promote the game of chess while supporting charitable causes, empowering communities, and fostering a love for the game in all who join us.
