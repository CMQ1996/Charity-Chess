Our Activities

Charity Chess Tournaments

Entry Fees: Organize local or online chess tournaments with entry fees going to charity.

Corporate Sponsorships: Partner with local businesses to sponsor the event, providing additional funds or prizes.

Celebrity Matches: Invite local celebrities or well-known chess players to participate, drawing more attention and donations.

Simultaneous Chess Exhibitions (Simuls)

Grandmaster Simuls: Host an event where a Grandmaster plays against multiple players simultaneously. Participants can donate to play.

Team Simuls: Have a team of club members play against a Grandmaster or a high-rated player. The teamâ€™s combined effort against the single player can add an exciting dynamic.

Chess Marathon

24-Hour Chess Challenge: Organize a continuous 24-hour chess event, where players or teams can sponsor hours of play. Donations can be made per hour played or per game won.

Endurance Chess: Challenge participants to play as many games as possible within a certain time frame, with pledges made for every game completed.

Themed Chess Events

Costume Chess: Host a chess event where participants dress up as chess pieces or famous chess players, with entry fees going to charity.

Blitz or Bullet Charity Nights: Fast-paced chess events with short time controls can attract a lot of players and spectators. Charge an entry fee and offer small prizes.

Chess Workshops and Lessons

Paid Workshops: Organize chess lessons or workshops for beginners or intermediates, with proceeds going to charity.

Private Lessons Auction: Auction off a private lesson with a high-rated player or a coach, with all funds directed to charity.

Chess Puzzle Competitions

Solve for Charity: Host a chess puzzle-solving competition where participants pay a fee to enter. Offer prizes for the fastest or most accurate solvers.

Daily Puzzles for Donation: Share a daily or weekly chess puzzle on social media, encouraging followers to donate to solve it
